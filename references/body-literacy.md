# Body Literacy Reference

Use this file when answering educational questions about periods, discharge, ovulation, spotting, period color, PMS/PMDD, pain, heavy flow, irregular or missed periods, contraception-related bleeding, puberty, perimenopause, or fertility-window basics. Keep answers cute and plain, but do not sacrifice accuracy.

## Tone Formula

1. Start with the body mechanism.
2. Explain common variations.
3. Give calm observation boundaries.
4. Offer to record the detail when useful.

Do not introduce shame words unless the user used them first.

## Period Length

Plain explanation:

> 经期 2 到 7 天都常见。7 天不是超纲，只是正常范围里偏长的一端。

Useful boundary:

- If the user is consistently 6-7 days and otherwise stable, treat it as their rhythm.
- If bleeding often goes far beyond 7 days, flow suddenly becomes much heavier, or pain changes sharply, suggest recording and asking a gynecologist.

## Dark Brown or Nearly Black Period Blood

Plain explanation:

> 经期刚开始或快结束时，血流慢一点，血液停留久一点，颜色可能变成深红、褐色或接近黑色。像身体在慢悠悠收工。

Useful boundary:

- Common at the beginning or end of a period, especially with lighter flow.
- Ask about odor, pain, fever, non-period bleeding, and whether this is new for the user.
- If it happens throughout the whole period and is new or paired with pain/odor/heavy bleeding, suggest medical confirmation.

## Vaginal Discharge

Plain explanation:

> 白带是身体自带的小清洁队，负责保持湿润、带走旧细胞，也会跟着周期变化。

Common variations:

- Clear, white, milky, sticky, slippery, or wet can be common.
- Transparent, stretchy, egg-white-like discharge often appears near ovulation.
- Amount can change with cycle phase, arousal, pregnancy, contraception, stress, and medication.

Useful boundary:

- Suggest care when there is strong/fishy/foul odor, yellow/green/gray color, clumpy cottage-cheese texture with itching, frothy texture, pelvic pain, painful urination, bleeding outside the period, or symptoms that recur.

## Ovulation and Fertile Window

Plain explanation:

> 排卵期是卵巢放出卵子的窗口。不是每个人都在第 14 天，周期长短不同，排卵日也会漂移。

Common signs:

- Egg-white cervical mucus.
- Slight one-sided pelvic twinge.
- Libido or mood changes.
- Basal body temperature rises after ovulation, not before.

Useful boundary:

- Calendar estimates are rough.
- For trying to conceive, combine cycle history, cervical mucus, ovulation tests, and basal body temperature.
- For avoiding pregnancy, do not rely on rough calendar estimates alone.

## Ovulation Spotting

Plain explanation:

> 排卵期前后少量点滴出血，有些人会遇到，常和激素波动有关。像身体在周期中途打了个小顿号。

Useful boundary:

- Usually light and short.
- If bleeding is heavy, lasts several days, comes with pain, happens after sex, or repeats in a new pattern, suggest recording and checking with gynecology.

## Period Pain and Pain Map

Plain explanation:

> 子宫收缩会带来常见痛经，但疼痛位置、强度和是否影响生活，都是很有价值的线索。

Track:

- Pain score 0-10.
- Lower belly, back, thighs, rectal pressure, pain when peeing/pooing, pain during or after sex.
- Whether pain stops school, work, sleep, exercise, or normal life.

Useful boundary:

- Pain around the start of the period can be common.
- New, worsening, severe, daily-life-disrupting pain, or pain with bowel/urinary/sex symptoms deserves gynecology review.
- Mention possible directions calmly: endometriosis, adenomyosis, fibroids, pelvic inflammatory disease, IUD-related pain in the first months.

## Heavy Flow and Anaemia Clues

Plain explanation:

> “量大”不是比谁更能忍，而是看它有没有影响生活、补给和体力。

Track:

- Changing pad/tampon every 1-2 hours, emptying a cup more often than expected.
- Needing two products together.
- Bleeding through clothes or bedding.
- Clots larger than about 2.5 cm.
- Period longer than 7 days.
- Tiredness, dizziness, shortness of breath, paleness.

Useful boundary:

- Heavy periods can be someone’s baseline, but treatment can help if it affects life.
- Heavy flow plus tiredness or shortness of breath can justify checking for iron deficiency anaemia.
- Heavy flow with severe pain, bleeding between periods, or pain when peeing/pooing/having sex should be summarized for medical review.

## Abnormal Bleeding

Plain explanation:

> 不在经期的出血、同房后出血、反复点滴出血，适合认真记录日期和上下文。不是先吓自己，是先把线索摆整齐。

Track:

- Date, amount, color, pain, odor, fever, pregnancy possibility.
- Whether bleeding happened between periods, after sex, after emergency contraception, or after IUD/IUS insertion.

Useful boundary:

- Bleeding between periods or after sex should be checked.
- Pregnancy possibility plus bleeding, strong one-sided pain, fainting, or shoulder-tip pain needs urgent medical attention.

## PMS

Plain explanation:

> 经前烦躁、乳房胀、困、馋、浮肿，很多时候和激素波动有关。不是你突然变难搞，是身体后台在调参。

Common mood notes:

- 心情很差、低落、想哭、委屈、很丧。
- 烦躁、易怒、焦虑、睡不好。
- 更馋、更困、乳房胀、头痛或浮肿。

Useful boundary:

- Track timing and severity.
- If mood symptoms are severe, affect work/life, or include self-harm thoughts, suggest timely professional help.
- Explain PMDD as a more intense premenstrual pattern that can seriously affect daily life; do not use it as a casual label.

## Missed, Late, or Irregular Periods

Plain explanation:

> 周期偶尔迟到很常见。真正有用的是看它是偶发，还是最近开始换了排班表。

Track:

- Expected start, actual start, stress, sleep, weight change, exercise, illness, travel, contraception, pregnancy possibility.
- Cycle gaps shorter than 21 days or longer than 35 days.
- Missed periods in a row.

Useful boundary:

- Irregular periods can happen around puberty and perimenopause.
- Consider pregnancy possibility if relevant.
- Repeated irregularity, 3 missed periods in a row, periods not started by 15, or irregular periods with weight change, fatigue, facial hair, acne/oily skin can justify medical evaluation for PMOS/PCOS, thyroid, metabolic, or other causes.

## Contraception and IUD/IUS

Plain explanation:

> 避孕方式会改写一部分出血节奏。它不是身体乱套，是外部设置加入了周期系统。

Track:

- Method: emergency contraception, combined pill, progestogen-only pill, injection, implant, copper IUD, hormonal IUS.
- Start date or fitting date.
- Spotting, heavier/lighter flow, cramps, missed or delayed period.

Useful boundary:

- Emergency contraception is time-sensitive; if the user is asking after unprotected sex or failure, encourage prompt local medical/pharmacy guidance.
- IUD/IUS can cause cramping or irregular bleeding early on, but severe pain, heavy bleeding, fever, unusual discharge, or pregnancy symptoms need care.

## Period Supplies

Plain explanation:

> 经期用品不是道德题，是舒适度、场景和身体感受题。

Options:

- Pads: easiest entry, good for sleep and early learning.
- Tampons: useful for sports or less external wetness; should not hurt or feel obvious when placed correctly.
- Menstrual cups: collect rather than absorb; reusable, needs fit and practice.
- Period underwear: good for backup, light days, or leak anxiety.

Track:

- Supplies running low.
- Leakage patterns.
- Discomfort or irritation.

## Puberty and First Periods

Plain explanation:

> 初潮后的头几年，周期像刚开张的排班表，乱一点常见。

Track:

- First period date.
- Cycle length, bleeding days, pain, discharge questions.
- What the user did not understand and wants explained.

Useful boundary:

- Periods commonly start around age 12, earlier or later can happen.
- If periods have not started by 15, suggest medical review.

## Perimenopause

Plain explanation:

> 围绝经像激素系统进入改版期，周期、睡眠、情绪、热感都可能一起变。

Track:

- Cycle changes, heavy or skipped periods, hot flushes, night sweats, sleep, mood, vaginal dryness.

Useful boundary:

- Often happens around 45-55 but can happen earlier.
- Heavy bleeding, bleeding after sex, or bleeding after menopause still needs checking.

## Doctor-Ready Summary

Include:

- Last period start/end and usual length.
- Average cycle length when available.
- Recent concern notes: pain score/location, heavy flow, abnormal bleeding, discharge alerts, PMS/PMDD mood pattern, missed/irregular periods, contraception changes.
- Original user wording when useful.

Tone:

> 我们不是去交作业，是带着线索去节省医生的破案时间。
